What's this?

Well, A collection of mostly accurate info (as of 15th July 2025), primarily but not specifically for UG freshers of IITKGP.

It has the following sections :

  • Fresher's Guide : A collection of pages to help you settle into IITKGP.

  • FAQs : Frequently Asked Questions. Please see this before asking questions in the group.

  • Admin : All the administrative info you might wanna know. Includes info about Fees, The college portal (ERP), and more.

  • Acad : Academics. Some of the available Resources, info about EAA, info about credits, etc.

  • Life in KGP : All the things that makes KGP, KGP. Excepting Societies.

  • Societies : Fancy way of saying clubs. Definitely see this to make sure you don't miss out on any info!

  • Contact us : Well, if you wanna contact us, and other information about us. Disclaimers/ToS/Privacy Policy are all here.

So this is a month later, over a dozen 2AM bursts of motivation, a heck of a lot of coffee, and this site is finally done.

Not really, I still wanna do a little bit more, but that isn't part of the primary plan, so yea, what I & we initially planned is done.

Here's hoping this will be just as h

elpful to you as we imagined.

---

Why did I do this though?

  • There's this quote : "A society grows great when old men plant trees whose shade they know they shall never sit in". Part of the reason is that : I wanted something like this when I joined IITKGP in 2024, but I found nothing, so this was my attempt to rectify for those who come after.

  • I know that this is one of the best ways for a lot of people to know me, and that does motivate me a bit too. That being said, it never was the primary goal : I wanted as many people as possible to help me in this, and I have done this under an alias (ASR) instead of my actual name.

  • I thought a lot of information wasn't available online, so this was an attempt to bring all that info online. [And don't get me wrong, it wasn't, but what I later learnt is 1) It is available but no one knows where it is, and 2) No one maintains such information repos. And my one request to you, the reader, is to think how one can change this? The current system is obviously not enough.]
