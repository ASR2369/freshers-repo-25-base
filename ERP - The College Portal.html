This is, like mentioned, the college portal of IITKGP. Pretty much most major activities you can do/see will be through this portal, including, but not limited to Fees Payment, Subject & course Registration, Academic & Personal details, Time tables, etc.

Guide to registering on the site : https://erp.iitkgp.ac.in/AdmissionERPInstruction.pdf

Typical Uses

These will be the stuff for which you'll likely use the ERP system for (Official Reg Instructions). If you're a new student, please complete all the requirements (See official docs, or New user menu below), only then will you gain access to the full erp system.

New User Menu (IMPORTANT)

From the official docs :

After logging in, select the “Academic” menu from the bar at top. Go to “Update and Print Student Profile” (New Admission) link and click on it. Please note down your tentative roll number and hall shown in the link. Update all the details and click on “Update” button to save the changes. After successfully saving your data take a printout using the “Print Profile” button. “Print Profile” and “Pay Fees” button would be enabled only after the data saved in ERP. Students shall generate “student profile” from the ERP site after logging in and obtain print out of the same. They should submit signed copy of the students profile at the time of registration.

Only after finishing all this (registration, profile filling out, payments (not necessarily fully though)), will you get access to the full erp system.


EAA Choices (IMPORTANT)

In the first semester, you will have to choose what your Extra-Academic Activity is going to be. This will be a 1 credit/sem course, and will typically have 1-2 classes (2-3 hrs) a week. Please check out EAA page for more info.

Pathway : Academic Section -> Students Menu -> Exercise EAA choices.

Document Verification (IMPORTANT)

After completing new user stuff, you might be required to submit documents for verification. This can be done here. Pathway : Academic -> Students -> Upload Supporting Documents (New Admission)


Paying Fees

Typical Pathway : Academic Section -> Fees Menu -> Fee Demand & Payment -> Generate Demand (Finish generating) -> Back to Fee Demand & Payment -> Make Payment

Official Resources : Payment Instructions | Registration Instructions

If you have a fee waiver or anything else, you might be required to do some other stuff too (feel free to explore).

For any other info in regards to fees : Fees & Financing Page

Subject Registration

Every semester, you will be required to register for all the subjects you will be taking during that semester. For the first two sems, you won't have any choice, but in later sems, you will be required to choose what subjects you want.

Typical Pathway : Academic Section -> UG Menu -> Subject Registration

Set Campus Network Password

The campus wifi networks (GUEST, STUDENT, ACADEMIC, CAMPUS) run on an username-password system. The username will be your roll number, and the password will be set by you in ERP (and can be changed too).

Log into ERP -> Other Actions -> Set Username & Passwords


Other Useful Features

  • Time Table => Pathway : Academic Section -> Time Table Menu -> Choose as needed . Both central (every dept) & your timetable will be there.

  • Curricula/Subjects => NEW GENERAL LINK (This is accurate of 30th June 2025). You can also see your general subjects info (along with minor & other subject requirements) in this pathway : Academic Section -> Subjects Menu -> Choose as needed. This might not be updated.

  • Your Academic Information => This is basically your profile in the college, and includes all your personal details & academic details too (CGPA, attendance, etc.) . P athway : Academic Section -> Students Menu -> Your Academic Information -> C hoose link as needed

  • Scholarship => Most scholarships will have to be done via ERP, and this is the pathway : Academic Section -> Scholarships Menu -> Application for Scholarship

  • Feedback => This is general feedback on all the courses you had in a sem. Will typically open in the last month of the semester, and you will have to fill it out to get your hall ticket. Pathway: Academic Section -> Feedback Menu -> Feedback Form

  • Hall Ticket / Admit Card => Both mean the document you will have to show before writing your midsem/endsem exam. Can be found at the following pathway (UNVERIFIED) : Academic Section -> Examination Menu -> Ex amination Timetable (For Students)

Notices & Information

Public notices & such will be uploaded on ERP (you will NOT be notified, so do check regularly).

Pathway : Login to ERP -> Other Actions -> View Apna (Internal) IIT KGP Notices -> Choose as needed (Usually Academic-UG)

Fake documents & notices often circulate in whatsapp groups, so if you want confirmation, please check the notices here as most information is posted here.

Academic UG, Admin, & Misc will be your most useful sections
