Financing

That is, how do you pay this?

Payment Instructions : As given on the college portal.


Structure

You have the option of paying the fees either as a lump-sum amount when the fees portal opens up, or via instalments. The nature or amount of instalments is unverified as of 21st June. Once information comes, this will be updated.


Waivers

Please ensure to update your family income certificate (& other relevant certificates) before paying your fees. Any available waiver should be automatically applied within a few days, and you can pay your fees after that.

If you paid your fees before your family income declaration certificate was updated in the college system, you have the option to revise your fees in the college portal. You will be required to update your certificate, and fill out a revision form, and to the best of my knowledge, any extra amount paid will be put as credit in your account for future semester payments (Unsure if it will be reimbursed to your bank account).


Loans

IITKGP has partnered with a bunch of different banks to provide student loans. The current interest rate is ____ .

All the necessary information will be there with the banking partners, and they can guide you through this. Do check out multiple banks to ensure you are well-informed

[Do note that if your family income is below 9LPA , no interest on your tuition amount alone will be collected for the first 5 years. Please check this document for the fine print (IMPORTANT).]

You can avail the option of student loans from banks inside campus (See Banks in IITKGP ), or from your own bank outside of campus (if available), or from banks who have set up shop purely for financing student loans.

Please check out Payment Instructions pg 4-6 for details on how to pay if you have taken out student loan.


Scholarships

Based on merit & need, certain scholarships are also available for students. Most, if not all these scholarships will have to be applied through ERP (College Portal), and not independently . You should receive mails and notices in ERP (check notice & scholarship section, both) for these too . Here is a non-exhaustive list of such scholarships with last date (if released), rough eligibility, and where to learn more information.

  • Inspire - SHE : Merit (Criteria varies by year). Only BS Natural Sciences Students. 60+20k PA. Application will likely be through College itself. Last date will likely be in September 2025. Learn more at Inspire .

  • Aspire : Need & Merit Both. Full scholarship (subject to renewals). Last date is Aug 1st 2025. Learn more at Aspire

  • Reliance (Company) : Need (15LPA max) & Merit (Exams & Aptitude Test). Upto 2Lakhs over Course. Max 5000 students. Last date unknown, likely September. Learn more at Reliance Scholarships .
