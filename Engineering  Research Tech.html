Space Technology Students’ Society

External Description : They are dedicated to explore space technology and astronomy by conducting lectures, events and workshops. - M

Internal Description : The official student body under the Kalpana Chawla Space Technology Cell, IIT Kharagpur , the institute’s nodal link with ISRO. spAts is dedicated to promoting awareness and interest in space science and technology through a wide range of initiatives. These include engaging activities, outreach programs like STAC (Space Technology Awareness Camp), and its flagship Asia’s largest astrotech fest, NSSC (National Students' Space Challenge), which features competitions, talk shows, and workshops with leading space experts. Beyond events, spAts actively contributes to academic research by conducting literature reviews and publishing review papers in the field of space science and engineering, fostering a research-oriented environment among students.

Selection Format : Task -> Personal Interview

Selection Time : Spring Sem (Typically Jan-Feb)

Autonomous Ground Vehicle Research Group

External Description : AGV is an interdisciplinary team aspiring to win the Intelligent Ground Vehicle Competition held at Oakland University and be involved in the Research work related to autonomous and mobile robots. - M

Internal Description : AGV, which stands for autonomous ground vehicle research group is a Research Group (RG) which researches (duh) work related to vehicles which operate without human intervention. There are 3 teams : software team works on the algorithms like optical flow, multi-agent control, computer vision, etc; embedded team does work related to making chips for the bots and integrating features such as scanning and control systems; and mechatronics team which creates a chassis (framework) for the bot. It's a very cool research group! I'd recommend everyone gives it a try when selections happen in 2nd sem (after midsems), regardless of if you want to join, as the tasks are very, very challenging but you will learn a crazy amount of info from it.
ARK members often collaborate with AGV members a lot in inter IIT if there's an aerial control PS.

Selection Format : Task (Team Dependent) -> Personal Interview

Selection Sem : Spring Sem (Likely March)

Selection Link (when & if applicable) :

Links : Facebook

Team KART

External Description : TeamKART is the official Formula Student team of IIT Kharagpur. They are a group of students who design, manufacture and test Formula style racecars and represent IIT Kharagpur in FSAE competitions. - M

Internal Description : TeamKART is a soc where people build cars that participate in racing comps, such as Formula Bharat. TeamKART's latest project is an electric race-car, and it consists of a huge teams of people (Battery, Mechanics, etc.) working on one part of the car, which we will then fully build into an EV.

Selection Format : Test -> Task (Relevant to the team you chose) -> Personal Interview. Three different teams : Electronics & Embedded Systems, Mechatronics, and Finance & PR.

Selection Time : Autumn (October) or Spring (Feb) Sem, unconfirmed.
