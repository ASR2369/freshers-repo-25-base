About Us

Who are we?

We are a group of Sophs who found the information desert about this campus extremely torturous last year. Yes, our sophs were extremely useful and like 80% of this info wouldn't be there without them telling it to us first, but one, there was a lot of things sophs didn't know; and two, it's limited in scope: asking questions to a single person & only getting the answer for you. Plus, what we learnt after coming here is that we didn't even know what questions we should ask.

So, this is our attempt at rectifying it, Inspired by the efforts of MetaKGP & its wiki.

Do note: This site was independently developed, and is not part of any group in IITKGP.  We do allow any group to promote our site, as long as they acknowledge that this site was made by us, and don't imply it was developed in any way by them.

The main person behind this is me, aka ASR.

Of course I couldnt have done it alone, so here are all those who directly helped make this a reality:

  • SS- Helped in Ideation, and bringing the concept to life, and also is a partner.  Helping make many of the upcoming pages.

  • AA- Made the "Welcome to SNVH-IGH" page & content, and helped polish the site. Helping make many of the upcoming pages.

And of course, special thanks to all those who helped in reviewing this site, giving suggestions & information, and helping publicize this. You guys are too numerous to list out, but your help has genuinely been invaluable, especially to freshies.
