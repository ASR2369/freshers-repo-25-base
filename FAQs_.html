Is the JoSAA medical certificate fine for IITKGP, given the different format?

Likely, but this isn't confirmed yet (We are trying to get it confirmed officially, as of 10th July 2025).

If possible , we would recommend you to get one more medical certificate in the format given by IITKGP, since that would ensure no issues at all.

That being said, some people did manage with the JoSAA format alone last year, and I don't think the IITKGP admin will be extremely strict in regards to this, but please try to get IITKGP style, until we confirm no issues.


What is passing certificate (or marksheet)?

If you're from CBSE, the marksheet you get for your Boards are both a Certificate for passing 12th, and the marksheet. Verify this by checking if the phrase "Certificate cum Marksheet" is written at the top of your document. Feel free to submit both of them.

Other boards often given two different documents -> A certificate & marksheet, which is why IITKGP asks you to submit both,

What is fees acknowledgement?

If Basically the receipt of the Fees paid by you. Typically it will be the full fees, but the JoSAA Seat Acceptance Fees Receipt is also fine.

This hasn't been verified with admin yet (10th July 2025), but given that many people will avail loans after coming to campus, I don't think so it's a requirement for you to have the full fees receipt => Hence why JoSAA Seat Acceptance Fees Receipt is also fine.

How many & what photos & ID do I need?

Passport photo of you from the last year is fine, even if it is not the one you gave for JEE. Typically 5 is enough (usually 3 is used up in the admission process), but if you want more, you can get them in campus.
If possible, have a couple JEE photos, but wasn't needed last year.

Please bring your Aadhar Card (original). Any other relevant certificates too (See Documents ).

Fees

Main Page : Fees & Financing

What is the fees structure?

See Fees & Financing page

Do I have to pay the full fees before coming to college? When is the last date?

I don't think so. Two reasons:

1. Last year, many of my friends only paid the fees after a week after joining college. Only the seat Acceptance fee was paid beforehand.

2. Student loans exist, and those are only taken after college starts. Hence people taking loans don't pay beforehand, so why do you think you have to?

The last date for payment is not confirmed, but as long as you pay within a few weeks, you'll be fine.

Can I pay my fees through X method ?

Please check out page 2 of IITKGP Payment Instructions Doc for this info. Basically, the following are available:

a. SBI Online Banking: Log on to SBI online banking portal and make payment

b. Other Banks Internet Banking: Log on to respective bank’s site and make payment

c. SBI Debit Card: Pay with your SBI ATM cum debit card

d. Other Banks Debit Cards: Pay with non-SBI bank’s debit card

e. Credit Cards: Pay with any credit card

f. SBI Branch: This is a walk-in facility by which payment can be made in cash/cheque.

How should I pay my fees ?

Please check out IITKGP Payment Instructions Doc for this info.

What scholarships/waivers are available to me ?

For waivers, please check out Fees & Financing page.

For scholarships, you will usually get a mail or an ERP update in regards to available scholarships. These are mostly merit-cum-needs, but there are some purely merit based scholarships. An example (incomplete) list is also present in the Fees & Financing page.

Just to be clear though, your education here is already highly subsidized, that's why merit scholarships are rare. Even if you pay the full unreduced fees, which is about 3L per annum; that's small compared to the 10L+ spent by IITKGP per student per year.

ERP

Main Page : ERP - The College Portal

What is ERP ?

The official college portal. See ERP - The College Portal page for all the relevant info.

As a fresher, you will have to use this portal to complete your academic profile, and paying your fees, along with subject registration.

How do I login into ERP ?

Please check out the How do I sign up? section of the ERP page. ONLY DO SO AFTER GETTING A MAIL FROM COLLEGE (mail address ends in iitkgp.ac.in)

I didn't get a mail from college ? Do I still try to login ?

No!

You will get an e-mail from the college AFTER you freeze your seat, and pay the seat acceptance fees. It usually (not always) takes a couple days after you do it, for the e-mail to be sent.

In case, it has been more than a week (or 20th July +), and you have yet to receive the mail, please contact the college Admin.

Can my parents come with me to the hostel? Can they stay with me?

Depends. Parents of the same gender (Girl - Mom, Boy - Dad) are typically allowed to enter and stay in the hostel till Admission date (24th July), but it might vary for you. Parents of opposite gender aren't allowed beyond main gate (unless you're lucky).

If staying, they might get beds in common room; and while not allowed, you can also try and have them in your room if your roomies are fine with it.

Induction (College Entry)

Relevant Pages : Fresher's Guide | First Day Timeline | First Week Timeline

What's the Induction Programme?

IITKGP's programme to help you transition to college programme. Tentatively 25-29th July 2025. You will receive a mail in regards to the schedule, definitely attend it.

Last years (as of 17th July 2025) induction website : Induction programme 2024

For my personal opinion, see Induction Programme subsection of the First Week Timeline page.

What is Admission Day? What do I need to do then?

Tentatively on 24th July 2025.

Your academic registration, i.e. registering you within the rolls of the college will happen on this day. It will typically involve document verification (See Documents ), and will take about an hour or so. Parents aren't allowed, and last year, it took place in the Vikramshila complex (See Important Locations - Fresher's Guide page).

You will get a mail in regards to this, don't worry. Follow it properly.

What is Reporting Day? What do I need to do then?

Tentatively on 22nd & 23rd July 2025.

You will finish your room allotment process on these days, but you can also finish it on the 24th of July too. For details on the process, please check the following links :

  • Boys' Room Allocation

  • Girls' Room Allocation

There wasn't anything else on these days last year, but things might change, so do check your mail.

Is there a Parent's Induction Programme?

I haven't confirmed this yet, but it will likely be there. Based on previous year trends, it will likely be on 24th or 25th evening.

Are my parents/guardians needed for any part of the process?

Nope!

I'd still recommend having them there for a few days : For support, and helping you transition.

What happens if I come late (later than 25th July 2025)?

As long as you are here within the month of July, you should be fine.

But we don't recommend this at all, because of the high amounts of hassle you will have to go through. If you come after the admission date, much of the process will have to be completed by you running here & there, and will take days instead of hours.

But if you have no choice, please contact your FacAd - Facility Advisior for what you should do. Their name & email will be available under the Student Information Link of Students Menu in ERP. Only visible after you complete ERP Registration.

Should I be worried about Ragging/Seniors/anything else?

Nope. Ragging is pretty much non-existent here given the extremely strict measures put in place in campus. In case, someone tries to do anything : Please contact the Admin, or a trusted senior (someone you know well), and it will be handled.

Seniors don't bite. In fact we'd like to help you as much as we can (if this website isn't a clue). But that being said, many seniors prefer formality and would prefer not to be treated like how you'd treat a friend, so just be respectful (not Sir or anything).
