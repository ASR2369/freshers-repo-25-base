Academic Resources

Incomplete, but still useful.

Like all colleges, there's a vibrant community for getting resources. Most of the stuff I got was via whatsapp forwards, and certain websites (including Illegal stuff lol, won't put it publicly). But over my 1st year, these were the most reliable resources I have found.

CSE 2020

The batch of 2020, specifically Gopal, a graduating student from CSE made this website to collect all the resources of his batch (i.e. CSE 2020). While some of the resources are outdated, with changes in curricula; and most 2nd+ yr courses being irrelevant; the vast majority of its 1st year courses resources are invaluable.

This genuinely saved my CG by like a point lol.

Anyways, link : https://garggopal2001.github.io/cse_material/

IQPS

A product of metakgp, this is a Question Paper Search (Intelligent Question Paper Search - IQPS) based on the Question Paper Repo of the central library of IITKGP.

Do note that some question papers are missing, but regardless, this is the largest repo of PYQ papers in campus.

IQPS website - https://qp.metakgp.org/

PYQ repo - http://10.18.24.75/peqp/ (Usually only works in campus wifi/library)

SWG App

SWG (Social Welfare Group), a soc within the campus, has built an app for this exact purpose too. They often have documents & pdfs that others don't have - especially Lab Record notes and such.

Search SWG IITKGP on play store, for this app.

KGPellence App

This is not an app made by the insti, but rather by a few of your seniors (not the ones who made this website).

Here are some of the ways it will be helpful :

  • Find the resources you need: KGPellence makes it easy to find the academic resources you need by providing you with a comprehensive directory of academic materials for all departments every year.

  • Explore Campus Life: Discover insights about various societies, hall info, scholarships, and departmental info to make the most of your college experience.

  • Wealth of Academic and Non-Academic Guidance: Access a wide range of resources like Blogs, TSG Info, Lingo and support to excel in both academic and non-academic areas.

Play Store Link (DNE in App store afaik, please check)

MetaKGP

You know what, I'll let them describe themselves:

" Metakgp is a loose association of engineers, hackers, artists, and students from IIT Kharagpur, who collaborate on various technical and non-technical projects. We celebrate freedom and openness; all our projects are free as in speech (and usually beer too). "

Very roughly, metakgp is a group of students - Alumni & Current; who build project & tools for students in Campus. Some of the most useful tools (for me) are as follows:

  • metakgp wiki - https://wiki.metakgp.org/w/Main_Page

  • Chillzone - https://chill.metakgp.org/ (what rooms are free)

  • IQPS - See above - https://qp.metakgp.org/

  • Periodic table of socs - https://soc.metakgp.org/

And this is not all, there's so much more which you can find here : https://metakgp.github.io/
