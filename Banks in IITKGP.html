Bank Branches

The campus has 4 major branches, with one of them just opening up recently.


SBI - State Bank of India

The largest bank of the campus, most of the campus processing services are tied through SBI. You can obviously use other banks without issue, but the general recommendation is this bank if you're opening a new account.  Typical Bank timings (10AM-1PM & 2PM-4:30PM, Mon - Sat), and service.

Located near the main entrance, near Puri Gate - Location

Canara Bank

Previously known as Syndicate Bank (and still known as that on maps), it's now a Canara Bank branch after the two banks merged. Timings are 10AM - 4PM, Mon-Sat.

Located at Location .

HDFC

The newest bank of the campus, this has opened up in the Nalanda Classroom Complex, next to Subway. Timings are 9:30AM - 3:30 PM, Mon-Sat.

Location .

Located centrally in TechMarket. Timings unknown
