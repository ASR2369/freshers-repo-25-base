What?

Most cultural associations have the following requirements :

  • Be a part of that state/culture, or at least have lived in the relevant state for a decent while (Even if you're from a different state).

  • Pay a Registration fee (usually 500ish (200-1000) per year).

  • Expected but not necessarily need to help.

In exchange, they organize the following

  • Celebrations of regional festivals

  • Food fests (internal & external)

  • Cultural Nights
