Awaaz (Journalism Hindi & English)

External Description : Awaaz, as said ‘The Voice of KGP’ is a media body of IIT Kharagpur, actively reaching to the entire KGP campus, students, faculties and to the alumni network. As an independent student organization, Awaaz brings out the latest happenings, intricate campus news, the debate on the issues of general interest, make a bridge between students, faculties and college administration. - Metakgp Wiki, Edited

Internal Description : Awaaz is a media organization active within the IIT Kharagpur campus. It was founded in 2006 by a group of students with the primary aim of keeping the IIT Kharagpur community informed about campus events and activities. Over time, its reach has grown significantly, and Awaaz has emerged as the most trusted source of news on campus. What began as a monthly magazine has now expanded to include social media and a website, through which we continuously keep the KGP community updated. The Awaaz team works tirelessly throughout the year to make this possible. As a team, Awaaz is a celebration of creativity and innovation, where one can contribute to domains such as Cartooning, Literary, Design, Analytics, Web Development, and Videography.

Selection Format : Task ( On Spot Writing/Cartooning) -> Personal Interview

Selection Time : Autumn Sem
