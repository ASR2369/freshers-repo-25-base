Life in KGP

So, this is meant to be your one stop shop for everything you need to know about life in kgp, other than stuff in Fresher's Guide - from food to extracurricular to facilities to locations and more.

Here's a list of available Pages:

  • How Not to starve: Where to Eat : A mostly exhaustive guide to all the different food options you have in campus, other than your mess. I'd recommend you try as many as you can, and not just decide on my descriptions : I like certain restaurants which a majority hate, and vice versa.

  • Weather : A short idea about the weather here, or in fancier terms : the moods of the temperamental mistress that is KGP.

  • Fests & Celebrations : All the vids you have seen about our fests? They aren't enough. You have to be here to truly experience it.

  • Gallery : Fests & Celebrations : That being said, here's a taste of what awaits you, thanks to @click_kgp.

  • Sports Facilities : An incomplete collection of relevant facilities for the more sports interested of our friend.

  • Socult Facilities : Socio-Cultural Facilities, along with everything else Extracurricular that isnt Acads or Sports.

  • Other Facilities : 3 pages aren't even close to enough for describing all the facilities of the campus, but doesn't mean we won't try!

  • A collection of our Locations : A list of some of the most commonly known locations in campus. Along with a treat for the first 5 to complete it.

  • Travel : Info about all the common modes of transportation within campus

  • Non-Campus Locations : Where do people go when they are fed-up of IITKGP?
