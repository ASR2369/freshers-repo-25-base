Important Dates - 2025


Admission & Induction Related :

Subject Registration Start - 16th July - A procedure you'll have to do online on the college portal ( ERP Page ), check What to do before coming here? page.

Reporting Date - 22nd & 23rd July - When you can come to campus & get a room. (Possibly finish Academic Registration too)

Admission Date - 24th July - When academic registration will happen, and you'll be admitted to IITKGP.

Induction Programme - 25-28th July - Programme by college to help you learn about everything here. Definitely see it.

1st year Classes - 29th July - 1st year classes start on this day
