The KGPian Game Theory Society, IIT KGP

External Description : KGTS is dedicated towards spreading awareness, knowledge and application of Game Theory. They are involved in publishing the half-yearly newspaper, "The Strategist" (This info isn't verified as of 2025). - M

Internal Description : The KGPian Game Theory Society (KGTS) is the first student chapter of the Indian Game Theory Society, dedicated to spreading awareness and promoting strategic thinking, while sparking interest in game theory among students. From weekly Game Nights and Knowledge Meets, the members discover the relevance of game theory in real-life and game-based scenarios. We also host several fun events like the Treasure Hunt and Stratathon, drawing enthusiastic participation from across the campus. Freshers should join to explore the fascinating world of decisions, strategy, and competition — all while having fun with like-minded peers.

Selection Format : Task -> Personal Interview. Management Team usually has direct PI.

Selection Time : Autumn Sem (Freshers). Vacation (Sophs).

Selection Link (when & if applicable) :

Links : Instagram

E-Sports Club

External Description : The campus club for e-sports, they organize many events & competitions for a variety of games, often in collaboration with Shaurya (Sports Fests Socs), and other socs.

Internal Description : Our society organises online as well as offline events : Last year we did 4 online and 2 offline events. Typically, there are weekly meetings where we discuss about our short term and long term goals. Overall it's a very fun experience to host events and play for your club.
[For the freshers (associate members) the society is divided into 3 main sections: core team, design team, tech team. Later on, the sponsorship and videography team will also be made from the best performers. For sophomores (executive head) there are many portfolios such as PR team, design team, sponsorship team, tech team, etc.]
Do note, joining our soc is easy, but staying here is what takes real effort!

Selection Format : Personal Interview -> A task as per your choice (core or design or tech. After evaluating your task, you will be sent the joining mail.
