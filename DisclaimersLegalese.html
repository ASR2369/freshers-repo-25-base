Privacy Policy

Given the static nature of this website, and the fact that it's supposed to be solely an information repo, none of your PII (Personally Identifiable Info) is collected directly or indirectly by us.

Certain anonymized , aggregate information will be collected with Google Analytics. This includes is but not limited to approximate location - city/country (via hidden IP address), pages visited, forms filled, etc.

Do note, your IP address will not be visible or available to us at any point, and any of the above data we receive will be aggregated (combined with similar datapoints for analytics), and anonymized (we will know a user saw X page, but not what user).

---

However, do note that this website was built using google services, and runs on their servers. They may or may not collect your information when browsing your site, and please check their & other 3rd Parties' privacy policies for this information.

---

We will collect any information you provide to us in the forms linked in this website, under this policy:

  • Any Personally Identifiable Information, PII (including but not limited to names, roll numbers, contact info, etc.), will not be shared beyond anyone directly involved in this project.

  • PII will be used for contacting you in regards to your question, and for anonymized data analysis. It will not be shared with any 3rd Party (excepting G-Forms, of course)

  • Any other information (Such as questions & answers) are not protected and may be used for any relevant purpose (Data analysis, FAQs, etc.). If the question has any PII, it will be deleted before being used, and if that's not possible, said question will be considered PII and/or deleted,

  • All the data collected will be stored on standardly secured accounts & devices of anyone directly involved in the project.

  • "Directly Involved" refers to people who have played a role in helping shape the project & have an active say on how it is being made, and does not include Question-Answerers, Publicity Helpers, or any other person in similar roles.

  • Non-PII data might be deleted at your request (Grievance Form), but final discretion lies with us after you submit said data.

  • PII data will be deleted asap upon request (Please give in writing via Grievance Form). We will try to delete all copies of PII at the end of this project, but no assurances can be made without a written request.

  • This data is collected via Google forms or other 3rd Party Services, so please check their privacy policies for any possible issues. We are not responsible for these.
